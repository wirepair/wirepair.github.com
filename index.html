Punking PunkBuster Presentation

OK folks, finally got around to releasing my slides from 44con. Probably the most interesting part of this talk was that I decided to create my own presentation framework using three.js. The slides themselves probably won't make much sense, so if you run a conference and want me to present at it, hit me up at twitter.

I got glowing reviews such as "doesn't suck" and "only drooled a little." As such, I believe it to be a pretty entertaining talk for most attendees.

Use the left/right arrow keys to transition from slides. No, you can't transition during an animation, so wait for it to finish. Check out the slides hosted thanks to github!
